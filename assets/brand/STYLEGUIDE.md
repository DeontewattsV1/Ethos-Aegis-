# Ethos Aegis Brand Identity Kit

## Core direction
Ethos Aegis should feel like an official-grade sovereign AI integrity defense system without copying or implying affiliation with any government, military branch, department, or protected insignia.

The identity is built around original abstract geometry:
- Fortified aperture
- Abstract shield containment
- Immune mesh nodes
- Halo-ring judgment field
- Severe symmetry and restrained typography

## Visual principles
1. **Institutional certainty over decoration**
   Use controlled spacing, disciplined typography, and restrained effects.

2. **Original symbolic language**
   Avoid real military seals, eagles, flags, stars, weapons, rank marks, and department-style crests.

3. **Black-on-black hierarchy**
   The identity should work in matte black, graphite, obsidian, and smoke, with steel blue as the primary accent.

4. **Operational clarity**
   Every mark must remain readable in GitHub headers, app icons, slides, documentation, and social avatars.

## Suggested typography
Use fonts with institutional weight and small-size legibility:
- Condensed heading: **Inter Tight**, **Archivo Condensed**, **Saira Condensed**, **Roboto Condensed**
- Technical UI: **IBM Plex Sans**, **Inter**, **Space Grotesk**
- Monospace accents: **IBM Plex Mono**, **Space Mono**

Do not package or redistribute font files unless you own the license.

## Safe-use note
These graphics are original civilian brand assets. They are designed to feel disciplined and high-trust, but they intentionally avoid protected government seals, military insignia, flags, eagles, stars, weapons, branch marks, and official department wording.
